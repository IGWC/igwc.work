The Candlelight Vigils Continue!

Sample Gates, Sunday, 11pm

Last Sunday, over 100 people joined the Candlelight Vigil to protest IU's restrictive "Expressive Activity" Policy! IU administrators attempted to disrupt the peaceful vigil, demanding that participants move to the public sidewalk by the Sample Gates where assembly is still legal. Many refused, and the Vigil proceeded with speeches, chalking, and songs. 

Join us, either on the sidewalk or by the Sample Gates, at 11pm this Sunday to show IU we won't accept this repressive policy.
