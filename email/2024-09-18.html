September 14 General Assembly Vote Results!

The General Assembly on September 14 introduced two measures to be voted on. The results of that general membership vote are:

98% approve our campaign to build to a multi-sector, 1-day action near the end of the semester to pressure to President Whitten to resign.

100% approve the proposed budget for this year.

5th Candlelight Vigil at Sample Gates, Sunday 11pm

Last Sunday, more students, faculty, staff, and community members gathered at IU's Sample Gates to expressly violate IU's repressive free speech policies. After making speeches, chalking, and songs, the protest marched to Dunn Meadow—the single designated free expression zone on campus, while others waited safely on the public sidewalk at Sample Gates. After hanging a banner on the fence that now surrounds the meadow, the gathering moved back to Sample Gates as IU police confiscated the banner.

Join us, either on the sidewalk or by the Sample Gates, at 11pm this Sunday to show IU we won't accept this repressive policy.
