Spring Organizing Meetings: Every other Tuesday at 5pm

Want to help us organize? Join our first Organizing Committee meeting of the semester at Canterbury House (719 E 7th St) and on Zoom (igwc.work/zoom) tomorrow, Tuesday, January 14th at 5pm!

Organizing Committee meetings are a great way to hop into organizing, get to know your fellow graduate workers, and help build a better IU!

This semester, Organizing Committee Meetings will happen every other Tuesday at 5pm. You can see a public calendar of these Organizing Committee Meetings and other events this semester at igwc.work/calendar.
