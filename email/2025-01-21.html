General Membership Meeting: February 1 at 4pm

Monroe County Public Library Auditorium

What is the difference between a General Membership Meeting and a General Assembly? A General Membership Meeting (GMM) is a space to discuss and think through questions as an organization, while a General Assembly (GA) deals with the mechanics: strike votes, budgets, etc.

On Saturday, February 1 at 4pm at the Monroe County Public Library (and on Zoom at igwc.work/zoom), we'll hold our first General Membership Meeting of the year!

Specifically, we'll be looking ahead at our goals for 2025 and talking through how to put ourselves in the best position to accomplish those this spring. If you are looking for a good place to hop into organizing, this is a great meeting to go to!

After the General Membership Meeting, join us for a Union Social at Atlas Ballroom at ~5:30pm to hang out and meet other graduate workers in the union! We hope to see you there!
